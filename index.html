Hi, I'm Kishlaya Jaiswal

Finance Analyst

I currently work in the finance sector, as an Analyst at Bank of America. Mostly my work includes validating models for counterparty credit risk.

Doing my graduation from CMI instilled me with a research attitude as well, so reading research papers (and perhaps be able to solve some problems) is something I really enjoy in my free time. My research interests broadly are complexity theory and graph algorithms. When I was working with Adobe, I also got quite interested in Topological Data Analysis.

Oh and besides all the academics, I am a (Vinyasa) yoga practitioner and I play Table-Tennis to keep myself moving.
